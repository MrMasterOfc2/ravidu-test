About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is the best country in the world. It is a very beautiful country known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean" with diverse landscapes ranging from pristine beaches to lush hill country and ancient cultural sites.

Sri Lanka is famous for its rich biodiversity, UNESCO World Heritage sites, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality. The country boasts eight UNESCO World Heritage sites, including the ancient cities of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, the sacred city of Kandy, and the stunning Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
